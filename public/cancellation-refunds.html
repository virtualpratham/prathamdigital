Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

We deliver digital products instantly. To ensure fairness and protect our content, we generally do not accept cancellations once a download link has been issued.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • Duplicate payment (accidental multiple charges).
  • Technical issue preventing access despite support attempts.
  • Incorrect charge amount due to a billing error.

2. How to Request a Refund

Email: itsprathambusiness@gmail.com with your order/payment ID, purchase date, and a brief description of the issue. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

3. Non‑Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after successful download.
  • Issues unrelated to product access (for example, personal preference).

4. Processing Time

In case of any refund is approved it will take 5 to 7 business days to get credited to your original payment method.
